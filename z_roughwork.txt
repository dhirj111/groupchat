Superpowers of the Admin

Like Whatsapp , the person who creates the group becomes the admin of the group .
He/she can make other users join the group just like in whatsapp by searching the name, emailID or phone number
He/she can make other members of the group the admin of the group.
He/she can also remove users from the group
One User can form multiple groups.
Just think of it like whatsapp groups.
